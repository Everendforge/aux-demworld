# Provisional Wand License

This laminated credential permits the holder to perform one plot-relevant spell under faculty supervision. It glows when used responsibly and emits a disappointed cough when waved theatrically.

[[Professor Beige]] awards it after Tess solves an exam by questioning the premise rather than choosing one of the four suspiciously faction-coded answers.
