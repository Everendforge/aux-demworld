# Mutual Aid Badge

The Badge certifies that its holder solved a creature conflict without forced capture, elemental exploitation, or an inspirational speech delivered before listening.

It is also a key to the [[Continuity Engine]], whose original designers considered consent a foundational compatibility protocol.
