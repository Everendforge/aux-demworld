# Red Stamp of Canon

The Stamp makes one declarative sentence true across the Grand Continuity. It cannot resolve ambiguity; it merely relocates the ambiguity into footnotes, spin-offs, and increasingly tense family dinners.

Tess is issued the Stamp when assigned to audit the [[Continuity Engine]]. Using it enables the Patched Canon ending, while refusing it strengthens Freeplot's argument that no person should own universal truth.
