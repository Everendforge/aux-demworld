# Stellar Plot Coupon

A holographic voucher redeemable for one maneuver that ignores fuel, distance, and previously established ship capabilities. The fine print requires an emotional confession during acceleration.

The coupon is kept in [[Orbital Cantina Nine]] until Tess and [[Captain Sol Ledger]] prove their heist is advancing character rather than merely filling runtime.
