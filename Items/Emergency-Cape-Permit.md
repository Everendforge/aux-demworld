# Emergency Cape Permit

The Permit authorizes one person to commandeer dramatic lighting, rooftop access, and a safe section of skyline during a declared continuity emergency.

Its reverse side lists prohibited actions, including anonymous property damage, unscheduled origin stories, and beams whose color has not been cleared with air traffic control.
