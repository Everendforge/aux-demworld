# Continuity Engine

The Engine is a city-sized machine beneath [[Finale Galleria]]. It does not decide which story is true. Its original purpose was to let contradictory stories coexist while preserving enough shared facts for people to meet each other.

The Ministry gradually converted negotiation into enforcement. [[Continuity Studios]] then added a reboot control disguised as a user-experience improvement.

## Four-key interface

The Engine recognizes four genre credentials: the [[Stellar Plot Coupon]], [[Provisional Wand License]], [[Mutual Aid Badge]], and [[Emergency Cape Permit]]. Together they grant access to the final continuity console.
