# The Present Convergence

The [[Continuity Engine]] begins collapsing all genre regions into a single market-tested timeline. Every prophecy assigns Tess Quill as temporary chosen one, and the Executive Producer schedules a reboot for midnight.

This is the historical event dramatized by [[The Last Acceptable Reboot]]. Its final canonical outcome is intentionally unresolved so the PathBranching endings can demonstrate alternate conclusions.
