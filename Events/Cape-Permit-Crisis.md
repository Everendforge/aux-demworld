# The Cape Permit Crisis

Seven simultaneous skyline beams caused rolling dramatic blackouts across Cape City. Local 404 negotiated a licensing framework that reduced collateral damage and guaranteed villains access to safer monologue platforms.

The [[Emergency Cape Permit]] was created as part of the settlement.
