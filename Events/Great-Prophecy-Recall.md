# The Great Prophecy Recall

Thousands of prophecies were recalled after investigators discovered they all selected the same brooding teenager. [[Nana Null]] led the repair effort and quietly added an opt-out field to destiny records.

The opt-out field remains technically available but is printed in invisible ink.
