# The First Merger

Budgetary pressure combined seven narrative realities into the Grand Continuity. The operation saved infrastructure costs but placed three dark towers, two chosen villages, and one cheerful creature preserve on the same commuter line.

The [[Continuity Engine]] was built to negotiate the resulting contradictions.
