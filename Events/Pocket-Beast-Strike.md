# The Pocket Beast Strike

Creatures across the Wilds refused random encounters until trainers recognized rest days, consent, and shared merchandising revenue. The strike ended peacefully when Sprig-Nine evolved into a labor negotiator during live arbitration.

The resulting agreement created the [[Mutual Aid Badge]].
