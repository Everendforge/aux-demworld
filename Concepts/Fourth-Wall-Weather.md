# Fourth-Wall Weather

Fourth-Wall Weather is the measurable influence of audience expectation on local reality. Forecasts include exposition fog, montage winds, grimdark pressure systems, and a forty percent chance of cameo.

[[Duchess Spoiler]] senses incoming fronts before anyone else, which is why she always carries an umbrella to emotional climaxes.
