# Canon Debt

Every forced correction to shared history creates Canon Debt: memories that still exist emotionally after being declared factually impossible. Enough debt produces ghosts, contradictory flashbacks, and citizens who remember attending their own origin stories twice.

The Ministry records debt as technical error. [[Nana Null]] treats it as testimony from erased worlds.
