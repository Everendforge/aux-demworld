# Genre Bleed

Genre Bleed occurs when the rules, tone, or budget of one narrative region leak into another. Minor cases produce talking swords in police procedurals. Severe cases cause a romantic misunderstanding to acquire orbital weapons.

The [[Continuity Engine]] manages ordinary bleed by negotiating local rules. The [[Present Convergence]] overwhelms that negotiation layer.
