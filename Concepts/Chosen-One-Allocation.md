# Chosen One Allocation

Destiny is a finite civic resource assigned by the [[Office of Prophecy Allocation]]. Applicants receive priority for tragic childhoods, symbolic birthmarks, and demonstrated availability during finales.

The system chooses Tess because her audit credentials give her access to the Engine, then invents a prophecy after the fact to make the assignment feel timeless.
