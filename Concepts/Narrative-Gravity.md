# Narrative Gravity

Narrative Gravity pulls meaningful people and objects toward locations with unresolved stakes. It explains why strangers meet in taverns, villains choose visible towers, and every train eventually stops near the protagonist.

[[Finale Galleria]] exploits the phenomenon commercially by offering discounted rent to prophecies with three-act structures.
