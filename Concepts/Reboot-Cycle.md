# Reboot Cycle

A Reboot Cycle collapses divergent history into one simplified timeline. Familiar roles return with revised ages, cleaner costumes, and selective amnesia. The discarded versions persist as [[Canon Debt]].

Natural reboots once occurred after catastrophic continuity failure. [[Continuity Studios]] has learned to trigger them deliberately whenever audience research reports excessive lore.
