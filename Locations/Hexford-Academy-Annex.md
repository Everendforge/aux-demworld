# Hexford Academy Annex

Hexford is an ancient school of magic operating from a modern office park because the original castle failed accessibility inspection. The moving staircases were replaced with elevators that still enjoy riddles.

Students sort themselves by preferred citation style. Professor Beige guards the [[Provisional Wand License]] in the Department of Applied Foreshadowing, where every exam question has already been foretold but the grading rubric remains mysterious.
