# Orbital Cantina Nine

Every booth in Cantina Nine hosts a different clandestine meeting. The house band knows only one suspicious melody but plays it in seventeen alien time signatures.

The [[Stellar Plot Coupon]] is stored in the manager's safe and may be redeemed for one impossible space maneuver. [[Captain Sol Ledger]] has a plan involving forged invoices, a ceremonial moon, and no fewer than two ventilation shafts.
