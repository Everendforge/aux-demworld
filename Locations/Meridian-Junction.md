# Meridian Junction

Meridian Junction is a vertical city built around the rail lines of [[Narrative Gravity]]. Trains arrive from enchanted kingdoms, orbital republics, creature preserves, wasteland suburbs, and Cape City, often on the same platform.

The skyline changes with audience expectations. Towers become castles during fantasy weather, starships during space-opera weather, and suspiciously affordable apartments during romantic-comedy fronts.

## Districts

- The Ministry Stack, containing [[Office of Prophecy Allocation]].
- Union Platform, where the [[Freeplot Cooperative]] distributes unauthorized maps.
- The Galleria Line, terminating at [[Finale Galleria]].
