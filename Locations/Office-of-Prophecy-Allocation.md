# Office of Prophecy Allocation

The Office assigns destinies, processes chosen-one appeals, and maintains a waiting list for dark lords seeking age-appropriate nemeses. Its lobby number display has shown **NOW SERVING: 3** for six centuries.

Tess begins [[The Last Acceptable Reboot]] here after every prophecy printer produces her employee number. A mislabeled staff corridor hides the first clue to Nana Null's ordinary emergency exit.
