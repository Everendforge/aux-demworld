# Collectible Wilds

The Wilds consist of grass routes, elemental caves, improbably specialized habitats, and health centers placed at legally mandated intervals. Creature encounters are voluntary under the [[Pocket Colossi Union]] agreement.

Visitors seeking the [[Mutual Aid Badge]] must help settle a dispute between a [[Bureaucratodon]] and the ranger office rather than defeat a conveniently ranked gym leader.
