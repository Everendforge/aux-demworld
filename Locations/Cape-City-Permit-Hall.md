# Cape City Permit Hall

Permit Hall is both city hall and an emergency-response headquarters. Its roof opens for dramatic departures, while its basement contains the Department of Secret Identities and a very public lost-and-found.

Tess must earn the [[Emergency Cape Permit]] by resolving a crisis without causing a third-act skyline beam. [[Paladin Patch]] considers this the hardest test in modern heroism.
