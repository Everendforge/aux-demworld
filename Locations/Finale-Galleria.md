# Finale Galleria

Finale Galleria is a shopping complex built around the exposed chamber of the [[Continuity Engine]]. Every corridor converges on the central atrium because [[Narrative Gravity]] has a retail partnership.

The mall contains abandoned sets from previous timelines, a food court that serves symbolic meals, and the flagship offices of [[Continuity Studios]]. During the [[Present Convergence]], its skylight becomes the mandatory beam through which the reboot signal will fire.
