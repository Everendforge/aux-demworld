# Creature Arbitration

A [[Bureaucratodon]] blocks the ranger office over unpaid habitat reimbursement. Tess and [[Sprig-Nine]] can listen, mediate, or attempt a traditional battle that immediately fails an ethics review.

A fair settlement earns the [[Mutual Aid Badge]].
