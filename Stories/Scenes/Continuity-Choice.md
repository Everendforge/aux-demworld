# The Continuity Choice

At the [[Continuity Engine]] console, Tess selects what kind of ending the world deserves. Route variables and earlier discoveries unlock different responses, but the Eternal Sequel remains available whenever a decisive conclusion feels too responsible.

The secret Ordinary Lunch ending appears only if Tess noticed the break-room exit during [[Prophecy Overflow]].
