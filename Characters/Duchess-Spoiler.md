# Duchess Spoiler

Duchess Spoiler enters every room knowing what will happen five minutes later and is furious that nobody appreciates the efficiency. She was raised by a prestigious order of masked antagonists who reveal family secrets during unsafe architectural conditions.

The Duchess serves [[Continuity Studios]] because the studio gives her advance access to every script. She admires Tess's refusal to perform destiny correctly and may help expose the [[Reboot Cycle]] if Tess proves that surprise has value beyond opening-week revenue.

## Tell

When Duchess Spoiler says, "This is not the part where I betray you," it is always technically true and strategically unhelpful.
