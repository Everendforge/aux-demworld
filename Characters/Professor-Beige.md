# Professor Beige the Terminal

Professor Beige is the only wizard to survive seven final battles by scheduling each one during office hours. His robes are aggressively neutral, his hat has tenure, and his wand doubles as a laser pointer.

He teaches Applied Foreshadowing at the [[Hexford Academy Annex]], where students learn that raw magical talent is no substitute for a valid [[Provisional Wand License]]. Beige knows the [[Continuity Engine]] was never meant to preserve a single perfect canon; it was designed to negotiate among competing versions.

"A prophecy is merely a spoiler with institutional funding."
