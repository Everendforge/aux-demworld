# Paladin Patch

Paladin Patch is Cape City's licensed emergency retcon specialist. She can temporarily repair broken physics, contradictory backstories, and costume seams, provided the incident is filed before midnight.

Patch carries the [[Emergency Cape Permit]], an authorization powerful enough to make public infrastructure behave heroically for one scene. She mistrusts [[Continuity Studios]] because every sponsored rescue adds a merchandising clause to the victim's medical paperwork.

## Limitation

Her powers solve immediate continuity errors but cannot decide which version deserves to survive. That choice belongs to Tess.
