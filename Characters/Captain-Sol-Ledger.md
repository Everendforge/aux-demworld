# Captain Sol Ledger

Sol commands the _Outstanding Balance_, a cargo ship famous for completing impossible runs and submitting every expense correctly. He claims to be the forgotten heir of a stellar dynasty, but three different dynasties make the same claim and all use the same promotional photograph.

Sol helps Tess steal the [[Stellar Plot Coupon]] from [[Orbital Cantina Nine]]. His instinct is to improvise; his secret gift is noticing when improvisation has quietly become a recurring pattern.

## Complication

The ship's navigation system believes it is Sol's sarcastic co-protagonist and refuses routes that lack emotional payoff.
