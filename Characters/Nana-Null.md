# Nana Null

Nana Null is a retired prophecy clerk who learned to erase names from destiny without erasing the people attached to them. She keeps forbidden drafts in recipe boxes and can identify a corrupted timeline by taste.

Years ago, Null helped design the negotiation layer inside the [[Continuity Engine]]. When [[Continuity Studios]] demanded one authoritative timeline, she hid an emergency option in the break-room vending machine.

## Secret

The supposedly mundane exit ending is not an illusion. Null built a small continuity outside the genre economy where people can have lunch without foreshadowing.
