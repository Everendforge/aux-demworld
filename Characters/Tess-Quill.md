# Tess Quill

Tess is a junior continuity auditor whose greatest ambition was to finish a fiscal quarter without discovering a prophecy. During a routine inspection of the [[Continuity Engine]], every destiny index in the building prints her employee number under **Chosen One, Temporary, Benefits Pending**.

She is observant, dryly funny, and dangerously competent at finding contradictions. Tess can restore the existing canon, release stories from centralized control, accept a lucrative villain contract, authorize a spectacular reboot, or discover that the universe has a perfectly ordinary emergency exit.

## Core conflict

Tess wants people to choose their own lives, but she also understands why a shared world needs enough structure to remain legible. Her strongest weapon is the [[Red Stamp of Canon]], which can make one statement true at the cost of generating three mandatory footnotes.

## Relationships

- [[Nana Null]] trained her to distrust prophecies with good branding.
- [[Duchess Spoiler]] believes Tess is wasting a marketable destiny.
- [[The Executive Producer]] sees Tess as both a threat and a promising junior executive.
