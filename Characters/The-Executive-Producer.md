# The Executive Producer

No surviving document records the Executive Producer's original name. They appear as a perfectly tailored silhouette surrounded by floating audience metrics and speak exclusively in confident revisions.

The Producer intends to trigger the [[Reboot Cycle]], compressing the Grand Continuity into a safer, younger, more merchandise-compatible timeline. They are not trying to destroy the world; they are trying to make it easier to pitch.

## Offer

Tess can become Vice President of Destiny if she stamps the reboot as canon. The salary is excellent, the dental plan covers fangs, and the contract has no final page.
