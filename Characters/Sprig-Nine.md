# Sprig-Nine

Sprig-Nine is a small moss-covered creature with antlers, static electricity, and a comprehensive understanding of collective bargaining. Trainers once classified Sprig as a starter companion; Sprig now identifies as a labor organizer who occasionally fits in a decorative carrying orb.

Sprig guides Tess through the [[Collectible Wilds]] and negotiates access to the [[Mutual Aid Badge]]. Its battle cry translates roughly to: "Friendship is not an unpaid internship."

## Ability

Sprig can evolve into whatever form a scene requires, but deliberately remains small because the travel allowance is better.
