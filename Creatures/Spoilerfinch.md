# Spoilerfinch

Spoilerfinches chirp the final sentence of any conversation they overhear. Most predictions are accurate, but the birds cannot distinguish a definitive ending from a sarcastic draft.

[[Duchess Spoiler]] keeps one as a companion and argues with it professionally.
