# Fussgriff

The Fussgriff resembles a pocket-sized griffin wearing the expression of an underappreciated stage actor. It repeats nearby dialogue with improved projection and refuses combat unless given top billing.

Fussgriffs nest in discarded title cards and can carry one small object or one enormous grudge.
