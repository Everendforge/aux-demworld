# Bureaucratodon

A Bureaucratodon is a broad, armored creature whose scales resemble filing drawers. It can reject an attack for incomplete documentation or return it to the sender with comments.

The specimen blocking the ranger office is not hostile; it is waiting for six months of unpaid habitat reimbursement. Resolving its grievance earns the [[Mutual Aid Badge]].
