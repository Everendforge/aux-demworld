# Continuity Slime

Continuity Slimes absorb minor contradictions and adopt traits from every version. A slime exposed to three conflicting costume descriptions becomes plaid, armored, sleeveless, and deeply tired.

The Ministry treats them as maintenance equipment. Freeplot insists they are oral historians with unconventional methods.
