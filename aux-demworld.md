# The Grand Continuity

The Grand Continuity is what happened when several perfectly serviceable genres were merged by a cost-saving initiative and nobody preserved the original backups. Star dynasties, spell academies, collectible creatures, caped municipal responders, post-apocalyptic homeowner associations, and prophecy offices now share one reality held together by the overworked [[Continuity Engine]].

At the center lies [[Meridian Junction]], where narrative gravity pulls every quest toward the same transit hub, food court, and suspiciously final shopping complex. The official history is maintained by the [[Ministry of Narrative Compliance]], challenged by the [[Freeplot Cooperative]], and monetized by [[Continuity Studios]].

The tone is adventurous, affectionate, and absurd. The world treats familiar genre conventions as civic infrastructure with budgets, unions, permits, audits, and deeply unnecessary merchandising opportunities.
