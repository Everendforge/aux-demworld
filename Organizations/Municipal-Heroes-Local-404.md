# Municipal Heroes Local 404

Local 404 organizes caped responders, dispatchers, sidekicks, bridge engineers, and citizens who point toward danger. Members receive health coverage, identity protection, and one dramatic rooftop conversation per quarter.

The Local controls the [[Emergency Cape Permit]] and refuses to issue it for battles whose primary purpose is improving a villain's skyline silhouette.
