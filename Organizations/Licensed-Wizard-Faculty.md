# Licensed Wizard Faculty

The Faculty accredits spell programs, regulates dramatic robes, and investigates plagiarism between ancient prophecies. Its members defend academic freedom while requiring seventeen signatures to summon a familiar.

The [[Provisional Wand License]] is their contribution to the four credentials needed to operate the [[Continuity Engine]].
