# Ministry of Narrative Compliance

The Ministry maintains the official timeline, registers chosen ones, and issues continuity citations to paradoxes parked in loading zones. It is slow, sincere, and terrified that one unregulated side quest could collapse public transportation.

Most employees want stability rather than power. The institution's failure is believing that a single approved story can represent everyone who lives inside it.

## Story route

Supporting the Ministry raises compliance and unlocks the **Patched Canon** ending, provided Tess gathers all four genre credentials.
