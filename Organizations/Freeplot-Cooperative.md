# Freeplot Cooperative

Freeplot is a decentralized network of side characters, cancelled villains, background citizens, and creatures who survived their original narrative functions. They repair abandoned subplots and publish continuity patches under permissive licenses.

The Cooperative wants to open the [[Continuity Engine]] so every community can maintain its own branch. Their plan is generous but risks turning shared history into incompatible local forks.

## Story route

Working with Freeplot builds solidarity and unlocks the **Stories in Common** ending.
