# Continuity Studios

Continuity Studios finances heroic infrastructure in exchange for adaptation rights, naming rights, and the right to replace any confusing citizen with a younger interpretation. Its research division turned the [[Reboot Cycle]] from a cosmic accident into a quarterly strategy.

The Studio's pitch is seductive: one clean timeline, universal accessibility, spectacular visuals, and no homework. The cost is every inconvenient memory that made the world specific.

## Story route

Cooperating with the Studio raises spectacle and unlocks **Prestige Reboot** or **Executive Promotion**.
