# Pocket Colossi Union

The Union represents creatures previously expected to fight strangers, live in tiny containers, and communicate through marketable syllables. Its contract recognizes evolution as a personal choice and friendship as a relationship rather than a combat bonus.

The Union awards the [[Mutual Aid Badge]] to travelers who resolve conflict without forced capture.
